Volunteer Nepali Teacher - Summer 2023

During the summer of 2023, I dedicated two months to a deeply rewarding volunteering experience in a Nepali school in Pyin Oo Lwin. I had the privilege of teaching Nepali language and literature to approximately 50 enthusiastic students in what was essentially a summer school program.

The curriculum focused on engaging the students with the rich tapestry of Nepali literature, exploring stories, poems, and cultural nuances. It was inspiring to witness their eagerness to learn and their progress over the two months. We also conducted examinations to assess their understanding and provide constructive feedback.

Beyond the academics, this experience provided invaluable cultural exchange and a deeper understanding of the local community. The dedication of the students and the warmth of the people made this volunteering journey truly unforgettable.
